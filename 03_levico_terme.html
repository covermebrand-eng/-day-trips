iii.

The day, paced.

Built around your real morning, the 30–40 min walk to the station, and a 1 PM lunch at home before leaving.

i.

Saturday — May 23.

Vicenza → Levico
Fri night
Pack

Pack the bag the night before.

One small overnight bag for the two of you — see the packing section below. Tickets stay on the phone. Backpack ready by the door.

9:00
Wake

Slow morning at home.

Breakfast at home. Coffee. No clock pressure — the train doesn't matter until afternoon.

1:00
Lunch

Lunch at home.

Eat properly so the only food worry on the road is snacks. No need to stress about restaurant timing in Levico — you'll arrive after lunch hours anyway.

2:00
Out the door

Walk from Via T. dal Molin → Vicenza Stazione.

30–40 min walk. Stop for snacks at a bar or alimentari on the way (panini, dry crackers, bottled water for the train).

2:40
Station

Arrive Vicenza Stazione.

Buffer time before the train. Validate paper tickets at the platform machines if you printed any (digital app tickets don't need it).

3:00
Depart

Vicenza → Bassano del Grappa.

Regional train, ~50 min. Quick comfortable run through Veneto countryside.

3:50
Transfer

Change at Bassano del Grappa.

Allow ~20 min for the connection. Bassano is a small clean station — easy to navigate. The Valsugana train to Levico departs from here.

4:15
Direct ride

Bassano → Levico Terme.

1h 16 min, no more changes. The Valsugana line follows the Brenta River up into the mountains — increasingly beautiful as you climb.

5:30
Arrive

Levico Terme.

Out of the station, walk ~10–15 min west on Viale Stazione to reach Bellavista at Viale Vittorio Emanuele III, 7. Mostly flat. Through the leafy spa-park area.

6:00
Settle

Check in at Bellavista. Drop bags. Freshen up.

Habsburg-era building, recently renovated, family-run by the Galvan family for 60+ years. Pool is open (heated, runs May 15–Sept 15). The spa center is yours if you booked anything other than the Economy room.

7:00
Walk + aperitivo

Stroll to the lake while it's still light.

~10 min walk down to the Lido. Sunset isn't until ~8:50 PM — plenty of golden-hour. Aperitivo at one of the lakeside bars. Spritz, olives, the works.

8:30
Dinner

Dinner in town.

Try canederli (bread dumplings), carne salada, or freshwater lake fish. La Taverna — Levico Lake on the water if you booked ahead, or Pizzeria Al Conte for casual. Pair with a Trento DOC sparkling.

Bellavista's restaurant is also a real option — guests rate the dinner buffet highly, and you don't need to walk anywhere afterward.

ii.

Sunday — May 24.

Levico → home for dinner
9:00
Wake

Get downstairs by 9:30 — breakfast cuts off at 10:00.

Bellavista's breakfast is the highlight per every review. Bacon & eggs cooked fresh, Trentino cured meats and cheeses, homemade cakes, fruit, a chocolate fountain. Take your time — it's the best meal of the trip.

10:30
Walk

Strada dei Pescatori.

The Fishermen's Path along the lake. Flat naturalistic trail, water on one side, mountains on the other. The full loop is 8.5 km (~2½ hours) — probably too much for the time you have. Walk the prettiest stretch (north shore), turn back when you feel like it.

Alternative: Forte Colle delle Benne — restored WWI Austro-Hungarian fort, ~45 min uphill walk, ~€4 entry, audio guides, panoramic rooftop. Probably more your speed than your wife's.

12:30
Snack

Light bite by the lake.

Coffee, panino, gelato. Don't go heavy — you've got the train and dinner at home tonight.

1:30
Hotel checkout

Back to Bellavista, grab the bag.

Standard checkout is by 11:00 at most Italian hotels — call/ask the front desk Saturday to hold the bag after checkout if you want to stay until afternoon. They almost always will.

2:15
Walk to station

10–15 min back to the train station.

Same path you walked in on, reversed.

2:45
Train back

Levico → Bassano → Vicenza.

Same route, reversed. ~2½ hours total with the Bassano change.

5:15
Arrive

Vicenza Stazione. Walk home.

30–40 min back to Via T. dal Molin. Or grab a taxi from the station rank if you're tired (~€8–12).

6:00
Home

Home. Dinner at home.

Whole evening in your own kitchen. Way more relaxing than another restaurant meal after a travel day.

Sunday alternative: If you'd rather eat one more meal in Levico, a 7:30 PM dinner in town → 7:52 PM is the last train you can realistically catch from Levico, which lands you in Bassano around 9:08 PM and home around 10:00 PM. Doable but late. The "back home for dinner" plan above is more relaxed.

Things worth knowing — for May 23–24.

  • Pentecost Sunday is May 24. Not a public holiday in Italy, but Levico can be slightly busier with Italian and German-speaking weekenders. Book La Taverna ahead if you want it.
  • Bellavista pool opens May 15. Heated outdoor, ~24°C. Yours is the second weekend after opening — should be quiet, fully operational.
  • Lake water still cold. ~17°C / 63°F in late May. Sit by it, walk by it — swimming in the actual lake is for July onwards.
  • Bellavista breakfast 7:30–10:00 AM. Be down by 9:30 to not feel rushed. Worth setting a soft alarm.
  • Hotel checkout by 11:00. Ask Saturday at check-in if they'll hold your bag until ~2 PM Sunday — they almost always will at this kind of property.
  • Last weekend train Levico → Bassano: 7:52 PM. If you ever decide to stay later for dinner, this is the wall.
  • Tourist tax at Italian hotels is a few euros per person per night, paid in cash at checkout, separate from your Booking total.
  • Apps to install: Trenitalia for tickets (validate by buying in-app), Booking for the hotel record, Google Maps with offline data downloaded for Levico in case mountain signal drops.
